The dividing line between AI and human roles falls between fact-based and judgment-based decisions. In medical imaging, AI with superior accuracy should be preferred. But justice is inherently a human quality. Chess provides a historical parallel: humans dominated, then human-computer teams excelled, then computers surpassed all. This pattern repeats across fact-based domains.
