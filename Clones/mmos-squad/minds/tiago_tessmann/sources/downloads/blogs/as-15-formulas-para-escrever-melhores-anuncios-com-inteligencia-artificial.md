# As 15 Fórmulas para Escrever Melhores Anúncios com Inteligência Artificial

## Why Your Ads Aren't Converting

According to Google and Meta, "the creative is responsible for up to 50% of the results of a paid traffic campaign." Most entrepreneurs overlook this critical aspect entirely.

If you're frustrated with your ad performance or ROI, start with fundamentals: learning to write compelling copy, strong promises, and effective offers.

## The 15 Formulas for Creating Ads That Sell with AI

Each formula works as a prompt for ChatGPT, Bard, or similar tools to generate headlines, video scripts, descriptions, and creatives for your campaigns.

### 1. AIDA
**Attention – Interest – Desire – Action**

Capture attention, spark curiosity, build desire, and end with a clear call-to-action.

*Example (Sofa Cleaning):* "Dirty sofa? See how simple cleaning transforms your space. Save time and book now."

### 2. PASS
**Problem – Agitation – Solution**

Identify the customer's problem, intensify the pain point, then deliver your solution.

*Example:* "Stubborn sofa stains? They could worsen. Our professional cleaning solves it!"

### 3. PAIS
**Problem – Agitation – Invalidation – Solution**

Add a step that disqualifies competitor solutions or DIY approaches.

### 4. 4Ps
**Picture – Promise – Proof – Push**

Help customers visualize benefits, promise transformation, provide evidence, then drive action.

### 5. FAB
**Features – Advantages – Benefits**

Explain characteristics, explain advantages, highlight end benefits.

### 6. QUEST
**Qualify – Understand – Educate – Stimulate – Transition**

Qualify your audience and guide them to action through education and motivation.

### 7. STAR
**Situation – Task – Action – Result**

Describe a process with clear beginning, middle, and conclusion.

### 8. ACCA
**Awareness – Comprehension – Conviction – Action**

Build awareness, establish understanding, create conviction, then drive action.

### 9. SLAP
**Stop – Look – Act – Purchase**

Direct formula to grab attention and move toward purchase quickly.

### 10. BAB
**Before – After – Bridge**

Show the starting state, the transformed state, and position your product as the bridge.

### 11. 5 Classic Objections
**No Need, No Hurry, No Desire, No Trust, No Money**

Create ads that overcome these mental barriers consumers face.

### 12. FOREST
**Facts, Opinions, Rhetoric, Examples, Statistics, Triads**

Use persuasive resources like data, rhetorical questions, and social proof.

### 13. Feel – Felt – Found
Acknowledge customer feelings, show empathy, share a discovery.

### 14. Storytelling
Tell a narrative with character, conflict, and resolution—particularly effective for video content.

### 15. PEIPA (Original Formula)
**Problem – Intensifier – Urgency – Social Proof – Action**

"Your sofa may harbor dust mites affecting your health. Our slots are limited. Hundreds already resolved this. Book now!"

## How to Use AI With These Formulas

Use prompts like:
- "Create a 90-character headline using AIDA format for [product]"
- "Write a YouTube video script in Storytelling format about [service]"
- "Create an Instagram ad in PASS format for [target audience]"

Clarity in your prompt directly improves results.

## FAQ

**Can I use these in any niche?** Yes, all are adaptable for physical products, digital products, services, and e-commerce.

**How many ads should I create?** More is better—quantity generates quality. Then A/B test to optimize.

**Is AI better than human copywriters?** AI accelerates the process, but strategic oversight remains essential. Combine both approaches.

**How do I create my own formula?** Analyze your best-performing ads and identify patterns that worked. That becomes your proprietary framework.
