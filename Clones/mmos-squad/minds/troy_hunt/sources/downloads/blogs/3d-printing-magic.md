# From Dreams to Reality: The Magic of 3D Printing, with Elle Hunt
**Date:** September 18, 2024
**Source:** https://www.troyhunt.com/from-dreams-to-reality-the-magic-of-3d-printing-with-elle-hunt/
**Category:** Communication Style / Misc

Troy Hunt reflects on his daughter Elle's remarkable achievement of presenting at NDC Oslo when she was just 11 years old. He notes that he didn't feel comfortable speaking publicly about technology until his mid-30s, making Elle's accomplishment particularly noteworthy.

## The Presentation

The core message emphasizes that Elle successfully taught "a room full of hundreds of technology professionals things they almost certainly hadn't seen before." Her presentation focused on 3D printing and its creative applications.

## A Memorable Moment

Hunt highlights a particularly memorable moment during the Q&A session, suggesting viewers "fast forward through to the 55-minute mark" to witness Elle demonstrating a 3D-printed catapult. The catapult launches projectiles generated from Chat GPT prompts into the audience, with Hunt noting his daughter "was having the time of her life" during this interactive demonstration.

## Reflection

The article celebrates both the technical innovation and the joy Elle exhibited while sharing her knowledge with experienced professionals in the technology field. The piece serves as a proud father's account of his child's confidence and ability to engage and educate a sophisticated audience at an international developer conference.
