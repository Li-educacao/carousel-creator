# Weekly Update 300
**Date:** June 18, 2022
**Source:** https://www.troyhunt.com/weekly-update-300/
**Category:** Communication Style / Misc

Troy Hunt shares updates from an ongoing road trip across Australia. After traveling approximately 2,000 kilometers, he has reached Melbourne, which was the original destination before the journey expanded in scope.

## Road Trip Across Australia

Hunt documents the trip through a Twitter thread, which he continues to update as the journey progresses. He notes this is an extended thread format partly to allow followers to mute updates if desired.

## NDC Melbourne

The upcoming week features NDC Melbourne, a significant event for Hunt as it marks his first return to an NDC conference since attending one in London in early 2020. He expresses enthusiasm about this inaugural Melbourne event.

## On the Road

During the journey, Hunt mentions listening to the Hardcore History podcast, describing it as engaging but requiring shorter listening sessions due to its demanding nature.

## References Cited

The update includes several notable items: a tweet thread documenting the road trip, the Hardcore History podcast series, the NDC Melbourne conference, and information about the DivX SubTitles breach involving 783,000 records with plaintext passwords from a 12-year-old incident. The post is sponsored by Kolide, promoting compliance solutions for remote-first environments.
