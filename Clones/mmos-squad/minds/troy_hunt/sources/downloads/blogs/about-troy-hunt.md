# About Troy Hunt
**Source:** https://www.troyhunt.com/about/
**Category:** Biographical / Identity

## Professional Identity

Troy Hunt is an Australian Microsoft Regional Director and Microsoft Most Valuable Professional (MVP) for Developer Security. He has held these recognitions since 2011. He regularly appears in press coverage discussing security topics and has testified before US Congress regarding data breach impacts.

## Pluralsight Author

Hunt works as a Pluralsight author, having published more than 30 courses on web security and related technologies. His courses are highly rated and cover topics including OWASP vulnerabilities, HTTPS, ethical hacking, and cloud platforms.

## Have I Been Pwned (HIBP)

His major project is founding and maintaining "Have I Been Pwned" (HIBP), a free service aggregating data breaches to help individuals determine if they've been affected by malicious online activity. The service runs on Microsoft Azure's cloud platform (later migrated to Cloudflare).

## Speaking & Training

Hunt regularly travels internationally delivering keynotes and conducting developer-focused security workshops at major technology events. He publishes schedules of upcoming appearances and speaker evaluations publicly.

## Corporate Background

Prior to becoming independent, Hunt spent **fourteen years at Pfizer**, with the final **seven years managing application architecture across the Asia Pacific region**. This experience exposed him to diverse technologies and global outsourcing operations, informing his later teaching.

## Location

Based on Australia's Gold Coast. Welcomes technical inquiries, press requests, and content corrections.
