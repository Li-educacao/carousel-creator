# Future of AppSec: Jim Manico on OWASP Top Ten Issues

## OWASP Top Ten Changes
Discusses major shifts in priorities and emerging threats.

## Access Control
"Automated CI/CD scanning misses the number one OWASP problem — broken access control."

## Logging & Alerting Partnership
"Good security logging and developer-security team understanding enables impressive real-time attack intelligence."

"When developers explain app functionality and security logging together with teams, they know what logs matter most."

## Pipeline Teams
"A dedicated pipeline maintenance team working cross-functionally with security proves more efficient than merged DevOps roles."

## AI Security Training
Significant demand for his course covering "prompt injection, model poisoning, and data theft vulnerabilities in AI systems."
